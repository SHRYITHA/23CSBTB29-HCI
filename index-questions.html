Q.14 What if the order is not delivered?

Ans. Listed below are potential resolutions for orders undelivered to customers or those marked as Return to Origin (RTO).

  • (b) For prepaid orders, upon receiving the order at our warehouse, we will contact you. You can either choose to have the order re-shipped to the specified shipping address or opt for a refund.
